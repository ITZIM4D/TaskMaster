Overview:
    TaskMaster is going to be a task management and stress reduction app that can be helpful to
    people that may be overwhelmed by tasks or just need help staying on top of things that 
    need to be done. This app will help achieve this by breaking down large tasks, tracking 
    progress/stress and visualizing data gathered
